1. Active Departure Rate

Description / Business Meaning: This widget displays the rate at which vehicles are leaving the drive thru, during an active period.
How it is calculated (formula or logic): The displayed value is the `activeDepartureRate` rounded to the nearest second, ensuring it's not negative ($\text{Math.round(Math.max(activeDepartureRate, 0))}$). It tracks a `targetValue` dynamically determined by current time, site-specific targets, and client targets. The gauge's `maxValue` is `target * 2$. The `interval` and `total` are derived from `selectActiveDepartureRate`.
Source of data: Vehicle presence detection and configuration from site and client targets. Data retrieved using `selectActiveDepartureRate` from `'@/stores/departures'`, `selectSiteTimeZone`, `selectTargetIntervals`, `selectSiteTargets`, and `selectClientTargets`. Topics: `/eyeq/active-departure-rate/`. Requires ROI configuration.
Frequency of update: Real-time, updated every minute.
